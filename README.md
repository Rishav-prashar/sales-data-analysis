# sales-data-analysis
Exploratory data analysis and sales insights using Python &amp; Pandas
 E-Commerce Sales Data Analysis

Overview
This project focuses on analyzing e-commerce sales data to understand sales trends, profit patterns, and customer behavior. The analysis answers key business questions related to monthly performance, product categories, customer segments, and profitability to support data-driven decision-making.

## Business Objectives
- Identify months with the highest and lowest sales
- Analyze sales performance across product categories and sub-categories
- Evaluate monthly profit trends
- Understand profit contribution by category and sub-category
- Analyze sales and profit by customer segment
- Study the relationship between sales and profit

## Dataset Description
The dataset contains transactional sales data from an e-commerce store.  
Key fields used in the analysis include:
- Order Date
- Sales
- Profit
- Category
- Sub-Category
- Customer Segment

## Analysis Performed
- Monthly sales aggregation and trend analysis
- Category-wise and sub-category-wise sales comparison
- Monthly profit analysis to identify peak profit periods
- Profit contribution analysis by category and sub-category
- Segment-wise sales and profit evaluation
- Sales to profit ratio analysis

## Key Insights
- Sales show clear monthly trends with identifiable peak and low-performing periods
- Certain product categories consistently contribute more to total revenue
- Profitability varies significantly across sub-categories
- Customer segments differ in both sales volume and profit contribution
- Higher sales do not always translate to higher profit, highlighting margin differences
